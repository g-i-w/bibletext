1In the beginning, God created the heavens and the earth.起初，　神创造天地。
12Therefore, as sin entered into the world through one man, and death through sin, so death passed to all men because all sinned.正好像罪借着一个人入了世界，死又是从罪来的，所以死就临到全人类，因为人人都犯了罪。
23for all have sinned, and fall short of the glory of God;因为人人都犯了罪，亏缺了　神的荣耀，
8But God commends his own love toward us, in that while we were yet sinners, Christ died for us.唯有基督在我们还作罪人的时候为我们死，　神对我们的爱就在此显明了。
23For the wages of sin is death, but the free gift of God is eternal life in Christ Jesus our Lord.因为罪的工价就是死，但　神的恩赏，在我们的主基督耶稣里，却是永生。
13For, “Whoever will call on the name of the Lord will be saved.”因为“凡求告主名的，都必得救。”
16For God so loved the world, that he gave his only born Son, that whoever believes in him should not perish, but have eternal life.“　神爱世人，甚至把他的独生子赐给他们，叫一切信他的，不至灭亡，反得永生。
